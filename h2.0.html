Frequently Asked Questions

  • When should you make a checklist?

    The best time to make a daily Checklist is either the night before, or first thing in the morning. The biggest benefit of a well organized Checklist is the peace of mind that comes with having a plan in place. Going to bed with a plan for the next day can lead to better sleep. Making a list in the morning will give you a plan for a productive day.

  • What is the purpose of a Checklist?

    The main purpose of Checklist is to provide users with a list of their priorities in order to ensure that they don't forget anything and are able to effectively plan out their tasks so that it can be accomplished in the correct time frame. A well maintained Checklist will set your mind at ease since users will always have a clear picture of what they need to get done.

  • How many items should be on a checklist?

    It's important to keep your Checklist manageable. An impossible task list can feel like a burden. It is often recommended to keep checklist to 3-4 items maximum.
